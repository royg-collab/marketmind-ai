# 📊 Impact Model – MarketMind AI

## 🎯 Objective
To simplify stock market understanding for retail investors using Generative AI.

---

## ⏱️ Time Savings

Traditional analysis time per user: ~30 minutes  
Using MarketMind AI: ~5 minutes  

Time saved per user: 25 minutes

---

## 📈 Scale Impact

For 1,000 users:

25 min × 1000 = 25,000 minutes  
= ~416 hours saved

---

## 💰 Cost Equivalent

Assuming ₹300/hour value:

416 × 300 = ₹1,24,800 saved

---

## 📉 Risk Reduction

- Reduces misinformation-based decisions  
- Improves understanding of financial signals  
- Encourages informed investing  

---

## 🚀 Scalability

- Can scale to 1 lakh+ users  
- Can expand into portfolio analysis and financial education  

---

## 🧠 Key Insight

The system improves **decision clarity**, not prediction — making it safer and more reliable.
